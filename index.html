Desabafo
Supportive Club

Desabafo Supportive Club is group that meets once a month to discuss various aspects of life such as family, friends, relationships, and work.

The meetings are held in delicious brunch partner restaurants where members can enjoy a delicious meal while discussing important life topics. The club provides a safe and confidential space for members to share their thoughts and feelings, and to receive support and advice from others in similar situations.

The goal of the club is to help members improve their mental health and well-being through open and honest discussion, good food and support from others. (chatGPT text)
